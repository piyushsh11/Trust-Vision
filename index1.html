Trust Vision

Ideology

Building trustworthy AI for SDG 3 (Health) and SDG 9 (Industry).

Tiny, invisible perturbations can flip life‑critical decisions. Trust Vision exists to expose that fragility, add guardrails (attacks, defenses, CI checks, monitoring), and guide teams toward safer deployment.

Problem statement

Standard vision models misclassify with imperceptible pixel shifts. In health, that can mis-triage patients (SDG 3); in industry, faulty QC can ship defective parts (SDG 9). We need practical attack+defense workflows and a way to fail builds when robustness drops.

What this project does

Offers an interactive lab to craft adversarial examples, visualize defenses, log misclassifications, and run CI guardrails on curated eval sets. Two pathways: pixel-based attacks and parameter-based attacks.

SDG 3 — Good Health & Well-being

Retinal/medical imaging misreads can delay or misdirect care.

SDG 9 — Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Vision QC lines can pass faulty parts or halt production under adversarial noise.
